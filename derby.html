• Sigma Chi Beta Omega - Philanthropy

The Huntsman Cancer Institute is an NCI-designated cancer research facility in Salt Lake City Utah. It was founded by John Huntsman senior, a Sigma Chi alumnus. The Huntsman Cancer Institute’s missions statement is “to understand cancer from its beginnings, to use that knowledge in the creation and improvement of cancer treatments, to relieve the suffering of cancer patients, and to provide education about cancer risk, prevention, and care”. They provide hope to those affected or currently suffering from cancer through their research, education, and care. 100% of all proceeds go directly to the research facility. The Beta Omega chapter of Sigma Chi has committed to raising $20,000 in funds for the Huntsman Cancer Institute. You can help us reach our goal and be the generation to end cancer by donating.
