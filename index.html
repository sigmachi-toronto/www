About

Located at 350 Huron Street here in beautiful Toronto, ON., we are the Beta Omega Chapter of the Sigma Chi Fraternity. Chartered at the University of Toronto and Ryerson in 1922 our chapter moved Sigma Chi to international status.

We've got awesome alumni who were and still are exceptional men. Take for example Ted Rogers, President and CEO of the Rogers Communications Inc, who was an alumni of this very chapter!

Sigma Chi is not just a Fraternity, it is a place for men to be exemplary. It fosters brotherhood and builds lifetime friendships as well as giving you the tools to excel in day to day life.
