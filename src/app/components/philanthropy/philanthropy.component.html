Derby Days

Sigma Chi Toronto's Annual Philanthropy Event

What is Derby Days?

Derby Days is a week-long charity event held by Sigma Chi Toronto. We organize and host a series of events and competitions among the local sororities. Through Derby Days, we raise thousands of dollars each year for the Huntsman Cancer Institute.




During the 2019-2020 school year, we've raised $11,000 of our $20,000 goal.
